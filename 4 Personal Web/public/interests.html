Serious Stuff

I enjoy studying about computer. For example, I have been curious about how computer works since I worked at a game company. And this game called Turing Complete really helped me understand. I have learnt how single electronic components form into complex ones, how machine codes take effect, and how to programme with assembly language to achieve small functions in this game. It was a wonderful journey.

After getting to know about SQL injection while learning database on the Java course, I have also been interested in the cyber security field. Hacking is the art of rule manipulation. I plan to explore this field in the future after building a firm foundation of the basic concepts.

In my work Book Notes, to identify users, instead of using mature package, I practiced generating temporary, random tokens and sending them through cookies to better grasp the security-related knowledge.

Though being a necessity instead of interest to many people in the world, I like learning English as well. With my mother being an earnest English teacher, I started learning English at an early age and have always been excited to explore more. Having got a 7.5 in IELTS exam in 2020, I have been keeping enlarging my vocabulary size as well as listening to and speaking more.
